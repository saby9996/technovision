Technovision Challenge 2017

Technovision is a sister concern activity of Project Innovation Contest 2018. It is an Intra-University Project Contest which will be organized by School of Computer Engineering, KIIT University. The main objective of it is to motivate the student community to develop projects with Innovative ideas which should support as an add-on to the human lifestyle. It provides a platform for students from different schools/institutes of KIIT University.
To showcase their technical skills and Innovation in solving real life problems with the help of technology.
